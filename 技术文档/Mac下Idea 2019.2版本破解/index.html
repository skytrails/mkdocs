    Idea 2019.2破解版¶

    下载JetBrainsIdesCrack-4.2.jar¶

    [jetBrainsIdesCrack-4.2.jar][http://d.w3h5.com/down/tools/JetbrainsCrack/JetbrainsIdesCrack-4.2.jar]

    路径配置¶

    • 将JetBrainssIdesCrack-4.2.jar放到IDEA安装Bin目录下
    /Applications/IntelliJ·IDEA.app/Contents/bin/JetbrainsIdesCrack-4.2.jar
    • 修改idea.vmoptions文件
    # 最后一行加入
-javaagent:/Applications/IntelliJ·IDEA.app/Contents/bin/JetbrainsIdesCrack-4.2.jar
